Pacific Northwest
Scala Conference

October 19th and 20th, 2013 in Portland, CA

Overview

Come join other Scala fans and aficionados in Portland, Oregon for 2 days of talks and open sessions.

The Pacific Northwest Scala Conference is a regional event focusing on short talks about a wide range of Scala-related topics, and will bring together Scala enthusiasts from both the Pacfic Northwest and other areas

Call For Proposals

The Pacific Northwest Scala Conference will be taking place this year in Portland, Oregon on October 19th & 20th. We look forward to engaging with Scala users and those interested in learning more about Scala, from the Pacific Northwest and beyond. We will be hosting a two-day event with one single-track day of talks and a second day dedicated to open, self-organized working sessions, discussions, and focus groups. If you have a topic you’re passionate about, perhaps an area of interest in which you've become your company's or user-group's resident expert, or a new approach you'd like to share, we encourage you to submit a proposal for a talk.

Areas of interest that talks might cover include (but are certainly not limited to):

  • Case studies & experience reports
  • Interesting patterns & designs
  • Reliability & fault-tolerance
  • Concurrency & distributed systems
  • Data management & large-scale data
  • Tools & techniques
  • DSLs
  • Functional programming
  • Migrating to Scala from other languages
  • Beginner focused topics

If you would like to give a talk at PNWScala 2013, please submit your information using this form. Talks should be 30 minutes in length and additional time will be provided for questions, so you don't need to account for that. Submissions are due September 7th and we will be selecting talks by September 21st at the latest. Please note that if your talk is accepted, you will be given free admittance (normally $100) to the conference. You may want to go ahead and register before we make selections (registration will be online very soon), in order to guarantee a spot. We’ll refund your fees if your talk was selected.

Location

How To Get Here

The Pacific Northwest Scala Conference 2013 is held at the Leftbank Annex at 101 North Weidler Street, Portland, OR 97227. The Leftbank Annex is easily accessible by bus and light rail service, as well as by bicycle due to its proximity to Portlands main bike corridor.
